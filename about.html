Based on Sustainable Development Goal 3.7 , Sexyverse is a educational project about sexual freedom and rights.

Sexual education shouldnâ€™t be just about STDs and Pregnancy, because sexuality is a complex matter and everyone deserves to be rightfully informed about it.

Pleasure, Consent, Privacy, Diversity, Safety, Pornography, and also Awkwardness are all topics that need to be discussed when approaching sex and sexuality. Each of them is represented by a land and a people in the Sexyverse. As every aspect of sexuality is equally relevant in order to have a healthy and balanced sexual life, in the same way each of the lands of the Sexyverse is fundamental for the balance of the universe.

The project is divided into 6+1 chapters (at the moment the prototype you are playing stops after the first chapter), each from the point of view of one of the characters and a concluding chapter in which the player can choose his or her preferred point of view. The interactive narrative is accompanied by educational mini-games, and very relevant are the story and events.

You will also find some of the characters on Instagram, as well as a main page (@sexyverse_xoxo) and a Spotify playlist

Sexyverse is a university project housed at the Politecnico di Milano for the Interactive Transmedia Narrative for New Media course.
